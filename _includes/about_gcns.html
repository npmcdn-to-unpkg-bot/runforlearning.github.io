Grayslake Cooperative Nursery School

Grayslake Cooperative Nursery School strives to be a quality preschool with a focus on socialization and a positive start to the school experience. It is with commitment and dedication from parents and teachers that children develop academically, emotionally, socially and physically in a nurturing, family focused, bright environment. With parental involvement, it is truly a unique experience and one that will start your child on the right path to a wonderful, educational experience.
