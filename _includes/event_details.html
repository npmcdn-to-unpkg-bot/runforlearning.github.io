Please join us in our premier {{site.name}} on Saturday, September 20, 2014 in Downtown Grayslake, IL.

With the help of our school families and the surrounding communities, we hope to promote a strong foundation to the beginning years of our children’s education. Please come out and join in celebrating our children.

Food and beverages provided to all race participants post-race as well as fun, educational activities for all ages!

This event will be a professionally timed event with each participant’s time available after completion of the event.

On the day of the race there will be a period of open registration from 7am to 8am. The cost for registration on the day of the event will be $30.00.

5K will start at 8:30 with the 1K starting immediately at the conclusion of the 5K (around 9:30). Kids Dash will be at the conclusion of the 1K.

We will have an award ceremony at 10:00 AM Age groups 10 & under and then 5 year age groups up to 65+.
