Westlake Christian Academy

Our mission is to provide for Christian families a Christ-centered education that disciples our students to honor Jesus Christ in thought, word, and deed.

In our Preschool program, we believe that young children learn best through play and hands-on learning experiences which take place in a safe and caring environment. It is our goal to help each child grow and develop intellectually, physically, spiritually, socially, and emotionally. We provide a variety of developmentally appropriate learning activities each day including free play, circle time, center activities, and indoor/outdoor large muscle play.
