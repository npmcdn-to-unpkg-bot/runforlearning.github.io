Apex Fitness Couch to 5K

Has it been a while since you laced up your trainers? Feel a bit daunted at the thought of running a 5K?

Well don't worry — Apex fitness are offering a couch to 5K program, through Grayslake Park District (GLPD), that will prepare you to run the {{ site.running }} {{ site.org_name }} 5K.

A USA Track & Field certified couch and personal trainer will work with you to accomplish your goal of running either your first 5K or your best 5K.

To register for the program visit the Grayslake Park District Recreation Center at 240 Commerce Drive, Grayslake, IL. Alternatively download the {{ site.couch_to_5k_link }} and read about registration options on page 67.
