Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteer opportunities include course marshals, timers, day-of registration assistants, start/finish line assistants, water table and more. These are great for individuals or groups.

We are interested in people of all ages. Teenagers get high school credit when you become a volunteer!

How to become a volunteer

It is easy to become a volunteer for our event and incredibly rewarding too. See below for three convenient methods to let us know that you want to be a volunteer.

Online

We're using SignUp.com to organize our volunteer spots.

Here's how it works in 3 easy steps:

  1. Click this link to see our SignUp on SignUp.com
  2. Review the options listed and choose the spot(s) you like.
  3. Sign up! It's Easy - you will NOT need to register an account or keep a password on SignUp.com.

Note: SignUp.com does not share your email address with anyone. If you prefer not to use your email address, please contact us by email or phone and we can sign you up manually.
