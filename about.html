About Run for Learning 5K

A fundraiser to benefit four Grayslake area pre-schools — Grayslake Cooperative Nursery School, Gloryland Pre-School, Westlake Christian Academy and Peppermint Stick Children's Center.

Run for Learning 5K is a fundraiser to benefit four Grayslake area pre-schools

The inaugural running of the race will be in downtown Grayslake on September 9th, 2014
